The formula for a point on the sphere
R^2 = x^2 + y^2 + z^2;

The formula for a point inside the sphere
R^2 < x^2 + y^2 + z^2;

If the sphere center is not at (Cx, Cy, Cz) instead of the origin:
(x - Cx)^2 + (y - Cy)^2  (z - Cz)^2 = r^2


In terms of point and center:
Where P=(x,y,z) and C = (Cx, Cy, Cz)
(x - Cx)^2 + (y - Cy)^2  (z - Cz)^2 = (P - C) * (P-C)
